KWANDA INITIATIVE

Kwanda Initiative is a team of experts focused on developing national resources by providing solutions to Rwandan community.

Learn More

AGRICULTURE

Kwanda Initiative has expertise in the agriculture sector, which includes crop production, animal husbandry, agribusiness, and value chain development. They work with farmers and agribusinesses to increase productivity, improve product quality, and facilitate market linkages. Their services in agriculture include project development, consultancy, and connecting clients to investment opportunities and technology.

CREATIVE INDUSTRY

Kwanda Initiative has expertise in the creative industry, which includes areas such as design, media, entertainment, and arts. They help creative entrepreneurs and businesses to develop their products or services, improve their business models, and access funding or market opportunities. Their services in the creative industry include project development, consultancy, and connecting clients to investors or markets.

HOPSITALITY

Kwanda Initiative has expertise in the hospitality industry, which includes areas such as tourism, hotel management, and food services. They provide consultancy services to businesses in the hospitality sector, helping them to improve their operations, marketing, and customer service. They also work on projects related to hospitality, such as developing tourism products and services, improving infrastructure, and supporting entrepreneurship in the sector.

TECHNOLOGY

Kwanda Initiative has expertise in the technology sector, which includes areas such as information technology, telecommunications, and digital innovation. They work with businesses and entrepreneurs in the technology sector to develop their products or services, improve their business models, and access funding or market opportunities. Their services in technology include project development, consultancy, and connecting clients to investors or markets.
